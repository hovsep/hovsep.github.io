Hello, World!

Welcome to my blog! This is a space where I'll share thoughts on software engineering, interesting problems I've encountered, and projects I'm working on.

What to expect

I plan to write about topics that interest me as a software engineer:

  • Dataflow and flow-based programming architectures
  • Distributed systems and their challenges
  • Developer tools and productivity
  • Lessons learned from building software

I'll also share updates on my open source projects, including F-Mesh, a Flow-Based Programming framework for Go.

Why write?

Writing helps me think more clearly. When you have to explain something to others, you quickly discover the gaps in your own understanding. It's a forcing function for learning deeply.

"The best way to learn is to teach."

Thanks for stopping by. Stay tuned for more posts!
